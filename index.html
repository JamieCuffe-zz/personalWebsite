Fun Side Projects


  1. Broke a World Record In charge of fun events for HackPrinceton, I contacted the Guinness Book of World Records and recieved the specifications for, and continued to actually break, the World Record for The Most Number Of Balloons Blown Up By a Group of People

  2. Documentary Filmmaking A compilation of a few of my documentary films I created in high school including one about the inspiring story of an ultra-marathoner. I have recieved 13 international film awards for two documentaries "I'm Too Young To Buy A Prius" and "Making Molehills Out Of Mountains" available by request.

  3. Snapchat GeofiltersAlways having fun messing around with Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, I finally got to put it to good use making snapchat geofilters. Two of my designs - one for a social house that I belong to called Cottage Club and another that was for Princeton's big yearly concert called Lawn Parties - went live. The others are still going through the Snapchat process.

  4. Hackcon Speech I gave a speech about how best to acquire funding for hackathons from your university. This speech was presented at Hackcon II, a conference for Hackathon organizers, in Feb 2015

  5. Princeton Bananas I was the co-founder of a facebook page called Princeton Bananas where we post pictures of fellow students eating bananas in a light-hearted and fun way to bring the community together

  6. Highschool Harlem Shake I organizes and executed my highschool's Harlem Shake video. My highschool, Kent Denver School, had a great time and the who high school participated.

Web Projects


  1. Swap Tired of searching friending, following and writing down 10 digits of a phone number? Swap allows you connect on all your favorite sites with one tap. Web app accompanies the iphone app in Beta

  2. Verifitas A consumer friendly database of the companies that have been indicted by the Federal Trade Commission for Data Security issues. Take a quick look before entrusting your data with these companies.

  3. WhoUTalkLike Type in a phrase. Do you talk more like Jesus, Hodor, Lil Wayne, Sarah Palin, Elmo, Napoloen Dynamite, or Dwight Schrute?!

  4. ilooklike Upload a picture of yourself. Find your celebrity twin using advanced deep neural net facial comparison powered by Wolfram Cloud.
