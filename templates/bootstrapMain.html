About Swap

Swap is mobile iPhone application that makes it easier than ever to connect with the people around you. With a single tap a user will be able to connect to another user on multiple social media and sharing platforms.. The theme is open source, and you can use it for any purpose, personal or commercial.

One of the most tedious processes of beginning a relationship with another person is the onboarding process of getting their contact information. No more having to type in the digits of a new friends phone number or search for them on Facebook and LinkedIn. With Swap, a single tap allows you to request to connect with a nearby friend..

Once they have accepted your request, Swap seamlessly connects you on all of the social networks that you care cohesive platform for engaging with the people around you.
